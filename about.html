Mission
Statement

Our mission is to help protect the Earth by providing affordable and durable items that can be used to replace common items known for contaminating our oceans.


Our oceans need us now more than ever as we face a global increase in consumption and waste products. Microplastics have become so common that the average person eating around one credit card's worth of plastic per week.


We hope to break the cycle of plastic consumption and inefficient means of recycling with our line of plastic-free sustainable products.
